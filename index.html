About Me

Hi, I'm oselotl – a photographer passionate about capturing moments that tell a story.

My photography journey began with a simple fascination: how a single frame can freeze time and evoke emotions that words often cannot. Whether I'm exploring new cities, attending concerts, or spending time with family, I always have my camera ready to capture the beauty in everyday moments.

I specialize in travel, concert, and lifestyle photography. Each photo in my portfolio represents a memory, a feeling, or a story worth sharing. From the neon-lit streets of Tokyo to intimate concert venues, I aim to capture the essence of each moment authentically.

What I Shoot

  • Travel Photography – Documenting adventures across the globe
  • Concert Photography – Capturing the energy of live music
  • Lifestyle & Family – Preserving precious everyday moments

Gear

I shoot primarily with a Sony mirrorless camera system, which allows me to stay lightweight while traveling without compromising on image quality. My workflow emphasizes natural light and authentic moments over heavy editing.
